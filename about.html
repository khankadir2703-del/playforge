About PlayForge

Created by: Mohammad Khan

PlayForge is a professional workspace designed for GDevelop creators. Our mission is to provide tools that help developers forge, publish, and monetize their games easily.

PlayForge Quick Tutorial

1
Create Your Account: Start by setting up your profile in the Account Settings to manage your developer identity.
2
Open Creative Studio: Click the "Open Creative Studio" button on the home page to access your main developer dashboard.
3
Publish Your Game: Go to the Developer Console and click "Publish New Game" to upload your GDevelop projects.
4
Track Analytics: Monitor your Total Impressions and Earnings in real-time through the Console Overview.
