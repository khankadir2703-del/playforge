About PlayForge

PlayForge is a high-performance web gaming console designed for developers to create, publish, and monetize games with ease. Integrated with the GDevelop engine, it provides a seamless workflow from creation to live deployment.

Key Features

  • Instant Cloud Publishing: Deploy your games to the web in seconds.
  • Revenue Dashboard: Track your impressions and earnings in real-time.
  • Developer Console: Manage all your projects from a single professional interface.
  • Firebase Integration: Secure authentication and real-time database support.

How to Use PlayForge

1. Sign Up: Create your developer account to access the console.
2. Create: Use our integrated GDevelop link to build your game mechanics.
3. Submit: Upload your game files through the "Submit Game" section.
4. Earn: Monitor your game's performance and revenue through the Revenue tab.
