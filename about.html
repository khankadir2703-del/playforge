About PlayForge

Founder: Mohammad Khan

PlayForge is a professional workspace for GDevelop creators to build and monetize games.

PlayForge Tutorial

1. Setup: Go to Account Settings to update your profile.
2. Create: Click Open Creative Studio to enter the dashboard.
3. Console: Use the Developer Console to track your "Total Impressions".
4. Logout: Use the red Logout button at the bottom sidebar when done.
