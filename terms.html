PlayForge

Monetization & Developer Terms

1. Revenue Distribution

PlayForge operates on a transparent 70/30 revenue share model. Developers receive 70% of all advertisement revenue generated within their game pages.

2. Content Policy

Games must not contain harmful code, excessive gore, or copyrighted assets without a license. All uploads are scanned for security.

3. Payouts

Earnings are updated in real-time. Payouts are processed when the developer reaches a minimum threshold of $50.00.
